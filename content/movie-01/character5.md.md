# Who is He?

Gordon is a lieutenant at the Gotham City Police Department and Batman's trusted ally in law enforcement. He is a rare figure of honesty and justice in Gotham's corrupted justice system.
## Relationships with Other Characters

1. [[character1.md|Bruce Wayne / Batman]] - His trusted partner in fighting crime.
2. [[character3.md|Harvey Dent / Two-Face]] - An initial ally who later targets him as Two-Face.
3. [[character4.md|Rachel Dawes]] - Collaborates with him in the justice system.
4. [[character2.md|The Joker]] - Constantly seeks to undermine Gordon's efforts to maintain the rule of law.
### What Happens to Him at the End of the Movie?

Gordon becomes the commissioner of the Gotham City Police Department and survives the Joker's chaos but is left with personal trauma when Dent kidnaps his family and he is forced to conceal Dent's crimes and blame them on his trusted ally Batman.
#### Famous Quote

> "He's a silent guardian, a watchful protector, a dark knight."
