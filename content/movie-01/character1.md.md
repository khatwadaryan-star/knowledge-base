# Who is He?

Bruce Wayne is the billionaire CEO of Wayne Enterprises who secretly fights crime in Gotham City as the superhero Batman. He is driven by the trauma of his parents' murder when he was a child.

## Relationships with Other Characters

1. [[character2.md|The Joker]] - His greatest enemy, who represents chaos against the order that Batman seeks to represent.
2. [[character3.md|Harvey Dent / Two-Face]] - An initial ally within the law who later becomes a tragic enemy Batman is forced to fight against.
3. [[character4.md|Rachel Dawes]] - His childhood friend and love interest whom he hopes to settle down with after retiring as Batman.
4. [[character5.md|James Gordon]] - His trusted ally in Gotham's police department.
### What Happens to Him at the End of the Movie?

He takes the blame for Harvey's death as well as Harvey's crimes as Two-Face, believing that the truth of Harvey's corruption will harm Gotham, and thus becomes a fugitive.
#### Famous Quote

> "Sometimes, the truth isn't good enough. Sometimes people deserve more. Sometimes people deserve to have their faith rewarded."
