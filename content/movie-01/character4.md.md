# Who is She?

Rachel Dawes is Bruce's childhood friend and love interest, whom she has told she will settle with when he retires from being Batman. Convinced he will not retire, she, as assistant district attorney, is dating Harvey.
## Relationships with Other Characters

1. [[character1.md|Bruce Wayne / Batman]] - Bruce's childhood friend and love interest.
2. [[character3.md|Harvey Dent / Two-Face]] - Her boyfriend who works with her in law.
3. [[character2.md|The Joker]] - She is targeted by the Joker due to her close association with Harvey and Batman.
4. [[character5.md|James Gordon]] - She works alongside Gordon in the justice system.
### What Happens to Her at the End of the Movie?

Rachel is tragically killed in an explosion orchestrated by the Joker. Her death has a profound impact on both Harvey, who descends into villainy, and Bruce, who is left more emotionally isolated.
