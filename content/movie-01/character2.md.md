# Who is He?

The Joker is an anarchistic villain who is not motivated by wealth or power, but by the thrill of chaos and dismantling social order.
## Relationships with Other Characters

1. [[character1.md|Bruce Wayne / Batman]] - Batman, as a symbol of order, represents the opposite of the Joker's values.
2. [[character3.md|Harvey Dent / Two-Face]] - The Joker seeks to corrupt Dent into becoming a villain.
3. [[character4.md|Rachel Dawes]] - The Joker uses her as leverage to torment both Batman and Dent and eventually has her killed.
4. [[character5.md|James Gordon]] - The Joker constantly seeks to undermine Gordon's honest efforts to maintain the rule of law.
### What Happens to Him at the End of the Movie?

The Joker is caught by Batman and sent to Arkham Asylum, but he still succeeds in corrupting Dent, which in turn forces Batman to take the fall for Dent's crimes and become a fugitive.
#### Famous Quote

> "I believe that whatever doesn't kill you simply makes you stranger."
