# Who is He?

Harvey Dent is the District Attorney of Gotham City, who is beloved due to his fight against corruption. However, after his girlfriend Rachel dies and half his face is scarred, he becomes the villain Two-Face, who chooses to murder or spare the people involved in Rachel's death with a coin flip.
## Relationships with Other Characters

1. [[character1.md|Bruce Wayne / Batman]] - An initial ally whom he later blames for Rachel's death.
2. [[character2.md|The Joker]] - Dent's enemy who manipulates his grief to convince him into villainy.
3. [[character4.md|Rachel Dawes]] - His girlfriend, whose death contributes to his downfall.
4. [[character5.md|James Gordon]] - An initially ally whom he too later blames for Rachel's death.
### What Happens to Him at the End of the Movie?

Two-Face kidnaps Gordon's family and prepares to flip a coin to decide the fate of Gordon's son. However, Batman intervenes, saving Gordon's son but killing Dent in the process. Dent's crimes are concealed by Batman and Gordon to avoid harming Gotham.
#### Famous Quote

> "You either die a hero or you live long enough to see yourself become the villain."
